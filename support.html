Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì What are binaural beats?

Binaural beats are an auditory illusion created when two slightly different frequencies are played in each ear. Your brain perceives a third "beat" frequency equal to the difference between the two tones. This can help induce specific mental states.

üéß Do I need headphones?

Yes! Headphones are absolutely required for binaural beats to work. Each ear needs to receive a different frequency, which is only possible with stereo headphones or earbuds.

üß† What do the different brainwave states mean?

Delta (0.5-4 Hz): Deep sleep, healing
Theta (4-8 Hz): Meditation, creativity, deep relaxation
Alpha (8-14 Hz): Relaxed focus, stress reduction
Beta (14-30 Hz): Active thinking, concentration
Gamma (30-100 Hz): Peak focus, problem solving
