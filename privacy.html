Privacy Policy

Binaural Synth

Simple Summary: Binaural Synth does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Your privacy is completely protected.

1. Information Collection

Binaural Synth does not collect any personal information or data from its users. The app functions entirely on your device without requiring:

  • Account creation or registration
  • Email addresses or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking
  • Any form of personal data

2. Data Storage

Any settings or preferences you configure within Binaural Synth are stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Is not accessible to the developer or any third parties
  • Can be deleted by removing the app from your device

3. Third-Party Services

Binaural Synth does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app operates completely independently on your device.

4. Audio Processing

All audio generation and processing occurs locally on your device. The binaural beat frequencies are generated in real-time and are not recorded, saved, or transmitted anywhere.

5. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13, Binaural Synth is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children, nor from anyone else.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

If we ever change our data practices in the future, we will update this privacy policy and notify users through the App Store update notes. The current version of this policy applies to all current versions of Binaural Synth.

7. Contact
